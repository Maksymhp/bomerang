What is dynamic market response?

Dynamic market response protocol is a set of automated mechanisms built into the smart contracts of the protocol, that will ensure that the market price of the $BOOM token is oscillating around the fair price of the asset, where fair price of an asset is derived from the backing of the $BOOM token and expected future growth
